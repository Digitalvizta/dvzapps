Odoo Employee Sequence App

The Employee Sequence App by DigitalVizta IT Solutions is a dedicated solution for managing and displaying unique sequence numbers for employees within your organization. This standalone application allows you to effortlessly add and track employee sequence numbers, enhancing your ability to manage and identify employees with ease. Key Features: - **Unique Employee Sequences**: Automatically generate and display unique sequence numbers for each employee, making it easier to track and identify employees. - **Enhanced Employee Records**: Integrates the sequence number into employee forms,list and Kanban views for clear and organized employee information. - **Server Action for Existing Records**: Includes a server action to generate sequence numbers for existing employees, ensuring that all records are updated with unique identifiers.

Features

Commission on Sales Order.

User can configure commission while creating sales order, commission will be given based on sales person.

Sales Commission on Payment.

While payment is done by customer commission will be calculated based on sales person.

Sales Commission on Invoice.

While validating invoice commission will be calculated based on sale person.

Sales Commission based on Partner, Margin, Product & Product Category.

You are allowed to give commission based on Partner, Margin, Product and product category.

Commission Access Rights.

Commission manager and user acceess rights, only manager can create and configure commission, and user can see commission line, and analysis.

Commission Analysis and Report.

User can see commission analysis and print commission report for specific sales person between selected dates.

Sales Commission Access Rights

User with "Sales Commission : Manager" can create and configure different types of commissions based on different conditions.

Sales Commission Menu

Under Sales > Configuration > Sales Commission menu manager can create different types of commissions.

Types of Commission Provided

Commission manager can define four types of commission from this module.

  • Standard Commission,
  • Partner based Commission,
  • Product/ Product Category/ Margin Based Commission,
  • Discount Based.

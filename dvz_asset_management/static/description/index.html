Odoo Asset Management App

This standalone application allows you to effortlessly add and track employee sequence numbers, enhancing your ability to manage and identify employees with ease.


Key Features:
  • Comprehensive Asset Management: Capture, organize, and analyze asset information effortlessly without complications.
  • Asset Movement Tracking: Track the entire history of an asset during its life cycle by capturing movements between different locations separately.
  • Deprecation Handling: Move deprecated assets to a virtual "Scrap" location instead of removing them, ensuring their history remains accessible indefinitely.
  • No Integration with Odoo Accounting: Operates independently of Odoo accounting applications.
